About Me

Hi! I'm a recent Computer Science graduate from FAST University with a passion for building interactive, user-focused applications. My journey in tech blends strong academic foundations with hands-on experience in developing modern, responsive web apps using technologies like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+), and Python. I've worked on projects ranging from AI-powered games like a 4x4 Tic-Tac-Toe with Minimax and Alpha-Beta Pruning, to visualizing search algorithms on grids, and even exploring P2P communication applications. I’m deeply interested in the intersection of clean code, usability, and performance optimization. I also take pride in continuously learning, refining my skills, and contributing to real-world solutions. Whether it's creating fluid animations with Anime.js, working on intuitive UIs, or optimizing user experience across all devices.I’m all in.
